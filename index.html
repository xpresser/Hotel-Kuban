“Kuban Resort and Aquapark”, the landmark at Sunny Beach, is situated in the very center of the resort on one of the main resort promenades. The hotel is the perfect choice for those who wish to discover and reveal the real character of the resort edging with the best beach (only 150m. away) on Black Sea coast. A few steps from the hotel, you can enjoy range of bars, restaurants and clubs or just explore the shopping centers around.

Kuban Resort and Aquapark” offers excellent dining facilities including a main restaurant, lobby bar, poolside bar and snack bar, where you can sample some typical Bulgarian cuisine. Moreover, a stunning waterpark, comprising of slides and three connected swimming pools and a game center, where you and your children could find plenty of entertainment that will enhance your experience to have an incredible holiday. Lastly but not least for your pleasant stay the hotel offers a fitness centre and sauna to relax.
